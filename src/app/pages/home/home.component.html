Instructions

The purpose of this site is to estimate the costs of owning a vehicle. You provide values of a previously owned vehicle or predictive values of a potential future vehicle. Based on your input, final costs will be given. Descriptions for each input can be found below. You are able to add multiple vehicles for comparison purposes. To begin, navigate to the calculate page.
